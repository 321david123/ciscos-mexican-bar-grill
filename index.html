Nuestra Historia

A neighborhood cantina where every plate comes generous.

Tucked into downtown Moline at 1231 5th Ave, Cisco's is the kind of sit-down Mexican bar & grill the Quad Cities keeps coming back to. The kitchen turns out grilled tacos, hefty California-style burritos, and chicken-and-rice plates piled with melted cheese — while the bar pours the margaritas regulars can't stop talking about.

Family-friendly and easygoing, we take reservations, fire up the grill for dine-in, and keep pickup and DoorDash humming for nights you'd rather feast at home. Pull up a chair — mi casa es su casa.
